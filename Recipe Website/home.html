History of Lasagna

Many historians have their own theories on the origins of lasagna. While some versions of the story may bear certain elements of truth, many don't know that lasagna was actually invented by the Egyptians to worship their gods. Although a there is a lot of debate on the exact date lasagna was invented, many have agreed to a general consensus that lasagna definitively existed by the year 2300 BCE. Specifically, this dish was made as an offering to the mighty cat god, named Guharfeelduh, at the sunrise of the first day of the new week. The egyptians believe that the offering would ward off evil spirit and promote health.

Unfortunately, the true recipe of lasagna has been lost to the passage of time. Ingredients

1 1/2 lbs Lean Ground Beef 1/2 lb Italian Sausage 1 Large Onion, chopped
2 Garlic Cloves, minced 1 Teaspoon Salt 1 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Tablespoon Parsley 1 Tablespoon Oregano 1 Tablespoon Basil
2 Cans (30 Ounces) of Chopped Tomatos 24 Ounces Cottage (or Ricotta) Cheese 2 Beaten Eggs
1/2 Grated Parmesan Cheese Mozzarella Cheese Lasagna Noodles

Preparation Instructions

<


1. Brown the ground beef, Italian sausage, onion, and garlic in a large skillet. Add salt, pepper, parsley, oregano, basil, tomatoes (with juice), and tomato paste into the skillet. Cover and simmer for 1 hour.



2. Cook the lasagna noodles. If you don't know how to do this, click here to see a tastier (albeit more difficult) way to cook lasagna noodles!



3. Combine cottage (or Ricotta) cheese, eggs, a pinch of pepper, some parsley, Parmesan cheese, and Mozzarella cheese.



4. Begin to layer noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture, and repeat. Finish off with a layer of noodles, finishing the top with a layer of Mozzarella cheese. Cover the noodles completely.



5. Cover the baking pan with tin foil. Bake at 375F for 40 minutes, then remove foil and finish baking for 20 more minutes. Make sure to check every few minutes during the last 20 minutes of cooking to see if cheese is burning.



6. Take lasagna out and let cool for 10 minutes before eating.
